About Us

This is a online shop that sells inline skates and parts for hobby enthusiasts.

Instructions and how it works:

The main page of this website is the store page which contains all the products that can be added to the cart to be purchased.

You would first need to make sure you select the correct size and then click on the add to cart button with the associated product. Every item is added to the cart at the bottom of the page with the total of all the items added up plus sales tax. You can also remove items in the cart by clicking on the red X button. The cart counter in the top will show how many unique products are in the cart currently.

Once you have are ready to checkout, please click the checkout button which will clear the cart and process the order.

FAQ

Is there free shipping?

Yes, we offer free shipping. Please select the free shipping option in the cart by clicking the option(radio option button).

How long does shipping take?

Standard free shipping will take up to 5 business days in the continental United States.

Is there faster shipping?

Yes, we offer express or fast shipping. Please select the corresponding option in the cart by clicking the fast shipping option(radio option button).

How long does fast shipping take and how much will it be?

It's faster and will cost $9.99. You'll see it in the checkout price and final total.
